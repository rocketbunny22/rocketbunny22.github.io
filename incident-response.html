Incident Response

Rapid response and recovery services when security incidents occur. Expert incident handling to minimize damage and restore operations quickly.

When Every Second Counts

Security incidents can happen to any organization, regardless of size or industry. When they do occur, having a rapid, coordinated response can mean the difference between a minor disruption and a catastrophic breach.

Our incident response team provides 24/7 emergency response services to help you contain threats, minimize damage, and restore normal operations as quickly as possible.

Incident Response Process

1

Preparation

Develop incident response plans, establish communication protocols, and prepare response tools.

2

Detection & Analysis

Identify and analyze security incidents to determine scope, impact, and appropriate response actions.

3

Containment

Implement immediate containment measures to prevent further damage and preserve evidence.

4

Eradication

Remove threats from the environment and address vulnerabilities that enabled the incident.

5

Recovery

Restore systems and services to normal operations while monitoring for signs of persistent threats.

6

Lessons Learned

Conduct post-incident review to improve response procedures and prevent future incidents.

Types of Incidents We Handle

Ransomware Attacks: Rapid response to ransomware incidents with decryption support and recovery assistance.

Data Breaches: Comprehensive breach response including forensics, notification support, and remediation.

Advanced Persistent Threats: Detection and removal of sophisticated, long-term intrusions.

Insider Threats: Investigation and response to malicious or negligent insider activities.

Business Email Compromise: Response to email-based fraud and account takeover incidents.

DDoS Attacks: Mitigation strategies and recovery support for distributed denial of service attacks.
